Detailed Description

Constraint: Line lies on a circle.

This requirement enforces that a line lies on a circle. The constraint ensures that the line intersects the circle in such a way that all points on the line satisfy the circle's equation.

Convert the constraint to a function for solving.

The constraint ensures that the line satisfies the circle's equation:

\[ F = (x - x_c)^2 + (y - y_c)^2 - r^2 = 0 \]

where \((x_c, y_c)\) is the circle's center and \(r\) is its radius.
